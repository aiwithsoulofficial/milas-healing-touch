My Unique Path to Healing

30 years healing bodies. Then I went deeper.

I spent three decades as a licensed Physical Therapist specialising in orthopaedics, neurology and women's health. I know the body inside out. But I kept seeing patients carrying symptoms that had no physical explanation - patterns that wouldn't release no matter the treatment.

My own personal health challenges led me to explore unconventional healing methods when traditional medicine fell short. Today, I am living proof of the power of natural healing. I hold a dual Bachelor's and Master's of Sciences Degree in Physical Therapy and I'm always learning something new.

Every living organism has an innate way of healing itself. The key is to awaken and promote the ability to self heal.
