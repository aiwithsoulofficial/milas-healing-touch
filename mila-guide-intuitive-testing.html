The Sway Test

20 Minutes

Your body is a living pendulum.

Demo Now - Stand Up and Show Them
  • Stand upright, feet hip-width apart
  • Relax shoulders - loose but not sloppy
  • Close eyes if comfortable
  • State "Show me yes" - observe direction
  • State "Show me no" - observe opposite
  • Forward = yes, backward = no (for many, but NOT always - everyone is different)
  • The key: allow the body to respond - do not force it
Say This

"Allow the body to respond. Don't make it perform."

Practice Exercise - Guide Them Through This

Have everyone stand. Walk them through:

  • "Show me yes" / "Show me no" - find their direction
  • Test: "My body loves clean water" (should get yes)
  • Test: "I am sitting in a chair" (should get no, since they're standing)
  • Test with objects: supplement, crystal, essential oil, food
→

"Now let's add another tool to your kit - the pendulum. You can sit back down for this one..."

Pendulum Testing

20 Minutes
Open With

"The pendulum is NOT magic. It amplifies subtle energetic information. It is an extension of your intention, nervous system, subtle energetic communication, and intuitive guidance."

Demo Now - Hold Up Your Pendulum
  • Hold chain between thumb and finger, let hang still
  • Elbow supported, take a breath, centre yourself
  • "Show me yes" - observe (clockwise / counterclockwise / forward-back / side-side)
  • "Show me no"
  • "Show me maybe / unclear"
Important Teaching Point

Each person's pendulum language is DIFFERENT. There are no universal rules. They must calibrate their own pendulum first.

Practice Exercise

Have them calibrate their pendulum, then test:

  • "Is this supplement beneficial for me?"
  • "Do I need more water?"
  • "Do I have permission to continue?"
→

"Now we're going to take this to the next level - testing on another person. This is where it gets really powerful..."

Testing on Another Person

20 Minutes

Requires:

  • Permission - always ask first
  • Clear explanation - tell them what you're doing
  • Respect - gentle handling
  • Neutrality - no agenda, just listening
Say This

"You are not there to dominate their body. You are there to listen to it."

Demo Now - Basic Arm Test Method
  • Person holds arm straight out to the side
  • They resist gently
  • Apply LIGHT pressure only
  • True statement = muscle stays stable
  • False/stressful statement = muscle weakens/unlocks
Critical Point

NEVER aggressive. Too much force distorts results. Light pressure is all you need.

What can be tested:

  • Emotional stress
  • Energetic imbalances
  • Supplements
  • Healing priorities
  • Readiness to release
→

"Now I'm going to teach you something that opens this work up to a whole new level - surrogate testing. This is how you test for someone who isn't in the room..."

Surrogate Testing

20 Minutes

Testing through one person on behalf of another - person, animal, child.

Used when:

  • Not physically present
  • Child too young
  • Animal testing
  • Someone too weak to test directly
Teaching Foundation

"Consciousness and energetic information are NOT limited by physical distance."

Demo Now - Walk Them Through The Steps
  • Centre and ground yourself
  • Ask permission: "Do I have permission to surrogate test for this person/animal?"
  • Clearly identify who you're testing for
  • Connect only with their field for the highest good
  • Use your chosen method (sway, finger, pendulum)
Critical Boundary - Emphasise This

Surrogate testing is NOT for: spying, bypassing consent, satisfying curiosity, or checking up on people.

Say This

"That is not spiritual wisdom. That is energetic boundary crossing."

Teach this with maturity and integrity.

→

"Let's bring it all together now - find what works for you, and close with a powerful affirmation..."
