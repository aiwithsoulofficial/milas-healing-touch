The Higher Self, the Subconscious Mind & the Quantum Field

Understanding the three components that make energy healing work

In Mila's approach, three elements come together in every healing session: the Higher Self, the subconscious mind, and the quantum field. Each plays a distinct role, and understanding how they interact is what takes your practice from following a protocol to working intuitively.

The Higher Self

Your Higher Self lives inside of you and on the outside of you. It is your higher knowing. It lives in the quantum field of possibilities - where the Akashic Records live. Your Higher Self is like the librarian. It knows absolutely everything and can give you access to things that almost bypass your cognitive reasoning brain.

The Subconscious Mind

The subconscious mind is a program that keeps running inside of you. It is part of where your memories are, all of the experiences you have had, all of your beliefs. That program keeps running all of your life. You get those programs through your environment - depending on who your soul chooses as your parents.

Putting It All Together

You use the subconscious mind, the Higher Self, and a tool like the Emotion Code to help you find and release the energy. You put it all together to bring someone to healing.

"Using your intuition is a wonderful thing. Dr. Brad doesn't talk about the Higher Self, nor does he consider the Higher Self in the Emotion Code, but he does believe in the subconscious mind. I have a tweak to it."

Section 02

Connecting Higher Selves Instead of Taking Proxy

A different way to access information without taking on the other person's energy

In the standard Emotion Code, you take proxy of the other person - tuning in to their frequency and testing their energy on yourself. Mila's approach is different. Instead of taking proxy, she creates a gateway to connect her Higher Self to the other person's Higher Self in the quantum field.

"Instead of taking proxy of someone, I create a gateway for me to be able to connect my Higher Self to the other person's Higher Self. I use a totally different type of modality to do this."

- Mila Velazquez
What is Proxy?

Proxy means testing someone else's energy on yourself. You tune in to their frequency. With the intention of saying, "I'm going to be the tester for that person." You say the person's name and go strong. You are tuning into their frequency - like tuning into a radio station, FM and AM - and picking up what they are giving off.

Why Mila Stopped Taking Proxy

Taking proxy is taxing. By the end of the night, you are roasted. By connecting Higher Self to Higher Self instead, the same information comes through - through your intuition, your knowing, and your gut - without the energetic drain. No need for the ringaround of taking on someone else's energy.

When Mila manually muscle tests on someone else, the information flows through the Higher Self connection - it comes down through her fingers because she already knows the answer. It is a yes or a no, and her fingers change. People say it looks like a trick, but it is the Higher Self at work.

The Energetic Drain Question

If you are already connecting intuitively but still feeling wiped after sessions, it may not be your connection method that is the problem. As Mila told a student: "Perhaps what's draining you of your energy is not that you're not connecting to the person's Higher Self. Perhaps it's that you're not protected enough, or that you're not cleaning yourself off well enough."

Grounding, protection, connection - the foundation of every session

Section 04

Permission, Safety Checks & Interference

You cannot enter someone's Higher Self without their permission. This is non-negotiable.

Before connecting to anyone, Mila always asks if it is safe - for the client and for herself. She checks multiple times. And she always gets explicit permission before entering someone's energy field.

The Safety Checks - Before and After Connecting
  1. 1
    Before you connect: Ask if it is safe for you. Make sure you are okay, protected, and everything is good.
  2. 2
    Check for entities: Make sure there is no entity inside. It is safe to enter - then go in.
  3. 3
    Ask again: Once connected, ask the same safety questions again, just to make sure.
  4. 4
    Check willingness: Are they willing? Are they ready? Are they capable? Do they feel safe?
Permission Rules
  • You need the person's permission to enter their energy field
  • For a child, you must ask permission of a guardian or parent
  • For an animal, you must ask the permission of the owner - not the person who tends to them, the person who paid for that animal
  • Without permission, you will not get in
How to Recognize Interference

How do you know your manual muscle testing is off? When you ask a question and get a yes. Then you ask if it is in column A - yes. You ask if it is in column B - also yes. You know you are not in. Your Higher Self is showing you that you have some sort of interference.

"If you want to get into a child, you have to ask permission of a guardian or a parent. If it's an animal, you have to ask the permission of the owner of the animal, not the person who takes care of the animal."

- Mila Velazquez

Using Your Space to Find the Issue

When you are connected in the quantum field, the other person's Higher Self already knows what your space looks like.

Instead of digging for answers using only her own Higher Self, Mila asks the client's Higher Self to show her where the issue is - in her own physical space. Because when you are connected in the quantum field, their Higher Self knows what your space looks like. Their Higher Self will guide your Higher Self.

"Do you have a trapped emotion? Show me where it is in my space."

- Mila Velazquez
Step-by-Step: The Process of Elimination
  1. 1
    Ask to be shown. "Show me where it is in my space." Then begin muscle testing.
  2. 2
    Locate by direction. "Is it on my right-hand side? In front of me? Behind me? On my left?" Test each one.
  3. 3
    Narrow down to an object. "Is it in my Oracle cards? In my scribblings? My clipboard? Is it crystals? A book?" Go one by one until you hold strong.
  4. 4
    Work within the object. If it is a book: "Is it on the left side? The right? First shelf? Second shelf?" If it is a clipboard: "Is it in the tarot part? The questions I usually ask? Is it emotionally? Relationships? Career?"
  5. 5
    Land on the issue. Keep narrowing until you find the trapped emotion, then go to the Emotion Code chart. Find the emotion, release it.
Build Your Workspace Intentionally

The more tools you have in your space - reference books, Oracle cards, tarot decks, crystals, pendulums, charts - the more options the Higher Self has to guide you. You are not guessing. You are giving the Higher Self pathways to deliver information.

Section 06

Using Cards with Frequency Matching

The Pythagorean theorem applied to intuitive healing - the frequency of your question matches the frequency of the answer

Mila uses Oracle and tarot cards as part of her process of elimination. Her reasoning comes from an unexpected place: the Pythagorean theorem. A squared plus B squared is C squared - the frequency of your question will match the frequency of the answer, and the right card will come out.

"If we take a look at the Pythagorean theorem, A squared plus B squared is C squared. Therefore, the frequency of my question will match the frequency of the answer, and the card will pop out. I put that together and said, let me see if this works - and guess what, guys? It works each and every time."

- Mila Velazquez
How Mila Uses Cards in a Session
  1. 1
    Clear the deck. Clear any energy out of the deck before you begin.
  2. 2
    Ask a specific question. "Show me, in one card, what does [person] need to release for their highest and greatest good?"
  3. 3
    Let the card come. If nothing pops out, cut the deck - bring one half in front of the other - and pull the card.
  4. 4
    Read the card. Note whether it is upright or upside down. Use what comes through as the starting point for your next question.
  5. 5
    Ask around it. "Do you have a trapped emotion that's preventing you from [what the card showed]?" Then go to the Emotion Code chart and find the emotion.
Which Deck to Use?

You can use this with any deck. When Mila has multiple Oracle decks, she asks the Higher Self which one to use: "Which Oracle deck can you benefit from seeing something?" Then she tests each deck - Psychic Tarot? Crystal Spirits? Cosmic Journey? Past Lives? - until one holds strong.

Finding Issues, Getting Clarity, Getting Answers

Cards are not just for finding trapped emotions. Mila uses them to find issues, get answers to questions, find clarity - whatever she needs. If she has a question, she can find an answer this way.

Section 07

Asking Precise Questions & No Future Casting

Your questions must be exact, precise, and specific. Always yes or no. Never open-ended.

The quality of your questions determines the quality of your session. Mila is very clear about this: your questions are always exact, precise, and to the point. They are in the yes-and-no realm. They are not open-ended. They are direct.

Mila's Questioning Flow - From the Workshop

Here is the exact sequence Mila demonstrated:

"Do you have a trapped emotion today?" - Yes.

"Are you willing to release it?" - Yes.

"Are you capable?" - Yes.

"Are you willing?" - No.

"Is there something we can release in order for you to be more willing to heal?" - No.

"Okay, then what do you want from me?"

"Is there something else that we can release in order for you to be more willing?" - Yes.

"Is it you being confident?" - No.

"Is it you having a lack of belief that you can heal?" - Yes.

"Is that the priority?" - Yes. "Let's release it."

Then after releasing: "Are you now willing to heal?" - Yes. "Let's get going."

No Future Casting

The future can be changed. What it is today is today, tomorrow is tomorrow. You do not tell people their future. You make your future.

You can get clarity and guidance by asking questions - but you are asking what is for the person's highest and greatest good, not predicting what will happen. If something is not meant for the person's highest and greatest good to know, you will be shown that.

Guidance Questions vs Future Casting - Mila's Example

"Is it for my highest and greatest good to go on vacation?" - Yes.

"In May?" - No. "June?" - No. "July?" - No. "August?" - Yes.

"First half of August?" - No. "Second half?" - Yes.

"Is it safe for me?" - Yes. "Is it safe for me to fly to Mexico?" - Yes.

"To Tabasco?" - No. "Cancun?" - No. "Palma?" - Yes.

This is guidance - asking exact and precise questions to get clarity. It is not telling someone what is going to happen or making their decisions for them.

Section 08

Guiding Clients Without Telling Them What to Do

You guide them for them to figure out what they have to do for themselves.

Clients will ask you to make decisions for them. "Should I leave my job?" "Should I move?" "Am I going to get married?" Mila is clear: you do not go in to fix their issues by telling them what to do. You guide them so they can figure it out for themselves.

"I don't go in to fix their issues by telling them what to do. You guide them for them to figure out what it is that they have to do for themselves."

- Mila Velazquez
When Clients Are Wishy-Washy

Sometimes people ask because they truly feel something but cannot explain it. Other times, they are being wishy-washy and cannot make a decision for themselves, so they throw it in your hands. If you make the decision for them, they are going to blame you.

As Mila puts it: they are taking an easy way out. And then they are going to blame you.

What Mila Actually Says to Clients

When someone asks for guidance, Mila offers what she sees - from her perspective, her lens, her wisdom, her intuition:

"The feeling I get is that I think it's time for you to move forward. How you move forward, why you move forward, that's up to you. But I feel, from my perspective, it's time to move forward."

That does not mean it is what they need to do. It is what she sees from her perspective.

Helping vs Deciding

If someone needs a little assistance and asks "What do YOU see? How does this work?" - you can share what you get intuitively. But there is a difference between sharing your perspective and making their decision. Know the line. If you feel intuitively that someone is just here to dump the decision on you - trust that feeling too.

Section 09

Disconnecting & Cleaning Your Energy

When you are finished, you disconnect the same way you connected - with intention.

When the session is done, you do not just close your laptop or walk away. You disconnect deliberately. Mila follows a specific process to close the connection and clear her energy - every single time.

Mila's Disconnection Process
  1. 1
    Disconnect the bridge. Take the light away. Take away the bridge between you and the client.
  2. 2
    Send love. Send them love and peace and happiness as you disconnect.
  3. 3
    Check yourself. Make sure that you are good after disconnecting.
  4. 4
    Wash your hands. Sometimes scrub with salt. This is physical and energetic clearing.
  5. 5
    End-of-day full cleaning. Do your full routine - not only the space where you were working, but also your physical body. Cut cords. Clean the energy space. Make sure you do not have anybody's attachments.

"At the end of the day, not only where you were working, but also your physical - so that you don't have anybody's attachments, no cords. Someone can cord onto you because they think you're so cute and sweet."

- Mila Velazquez
Processing Time

After a session, the client may need processing time. Mila checks how many days the person needs to process - sometimes 1, 2, or 3 days. This is part of the closing: you confirm the processing time before you disconnect.
