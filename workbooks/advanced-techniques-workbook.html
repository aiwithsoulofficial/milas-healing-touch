Section 01

Setting Your Intention

Your intention is what starts everything. Without it, you are just going through the motions.

Before you start any session - with a client, with yourself, with a surrogate - your intention is what activates the work. It is not the magnet. It is not the chart. It is not the words you say. It is the energy you are putting out before you ever open your mouth.

Your intention comes from your heart. There are no magic words. There is no script. What matters is that it is strong, it is clear, and it is for the highest and greatest good of the person you are working with.

The 5 Pre-Session Checks

Before entering anyone's energy field, always confirm these five things. You are asking their higher self - not their conscious mind.

  1. 1
    Ready? Is this person ready to receive healing right now?
  2. 2
    Willing? Is their higher self willing to allow this work to happen?
  3. 3
    Capable? Is their system capable of processing and releasing right now?
  4. 4
    Safe? Is it safe - for them and for you - to proceed?
  5. 5
    For their highest and greatest good? Does this session serve their highest path?

If you get a weak response on any of these five checks, stop. Honor it. This is not failure - it is the system working exactly as it should.

Section 02

Creating Sacred Space

You are working in the quantum field. The space you create before you begin matters.

When you connect to someone's energy field - whether they are sitting across from you or on the other side of the world - you are meeting in the quantum field. Both of your higher selves come together there. That meeting point is your sacred space.

Mila has connected to children in the next room and adults on the other side of the planet. The distance is irrelevant. What matters is the intention you set and the energetic bridge you create between your higher selves.

The 4 Steps to Creating Sacred Space
  1. 1
    Set your intention. From the heart - not from a script. Be clear about what you are asking for. Call in your God, your angels, your guides. Ask for the person's guides and angels to be present with permission.
  2. 2
    Create light around yourself. Surround your own energy field with protection and light before you ever reach out to another person's energy.
  3. 3
    Build the bridge. Ask that your higher self and their higher self come together in the quantum field. Feel that connection. The information flows through that bridge.
  4. 4
    Create light around the client. Surround their energy field. Set your intention for what you do not allow into that sacred space - and hold it firmly.
What you are doing
  • Connecting higher selves in the quantum field
  • Opening the channel for information to flow
  • Establishing energetic protection for both of you
  • Inviting guides and angels with permission
What you are NOT allowing in
  • Energies that are not for the highest and greatest good
  • Entities or darkness that you cannot work with safely
  • Your own ego, projections, or assumptions
  • Anything that does not serve the healing

Section 03

The Process of Elimination

Your higher self and theirs are connected. Their higher self already knows everything in your space.

When you have asked every question you can think of and you still cannot find what you are looking for - this is where advanced practice begins. Instead of breaking your head trying to think of the answer, you let the quantum field show you.

Because your higher self and the client's higher self are connected, their higher self already knows every book, card, chart, and crystal in your space. You ask it to guide you directly to the answer.

"Show me and guide me to where the issue is in my space."

- Mila's words to the higher self
Step-by-Step Guide
  1. 1
    Ask to be shown. "Show me and guide me to where the issue is in my space." Then start muscle testing.
  2. 2
    Locate by direction. "Is it on my right side? Left side? In front of me? Behind me?" Test each one until you get a strong response.
  3. 3
    Narrow it down. Whatever is in that direction, start naming objects. "Is it my tarot cards? My oracle deck? A book? My crystals?" Go one by one until you hit strong.
  4. 4
    Work within the object. If it's a book - go to a page. If it's cards - let one come through or cut the deck. If it's a chart - work through the columns.
  5. 5
    Trust what comes through. When the word, image, or concept makes sense - even if it surprises you - that is your confirmation. The synchronicity is the science.
The More Tools You Have, the More the Higher Self Can Use

Build your workspace intentionally. Books, charts, oracle and tarot cards, crystals, pendulums, printed reference guides - each one is another path the higher self can use to get information to you. You are not guessing. You are giving it options.

Section 04

Reading the Body's Messages

Every symptom is a story. The body is not breaking down - it is communicating.

When a client's higher self shows you a physical condition or symptom - even one the client does not currently have - it is pointing you toward the emotional energy underneath it. The condition is the metaphor. Your job is to understand what it means energetically.

Mila's physical therapy background gives her a medical foundation for this work. But you do not need a medical degree - you need the language. When the colon shows up, you know it relates to letting go. When inflammation shows up, something is eating at the person. When the throat shows up, something is not being expressed. Start building that vocabulary.

Mila's Live Example - From the Workshop

Working with a student named Lori, Mila used the process of elimination and landed on colitis - which Lori did not have. But colitis meant: the colon (inability to let go) plus "itis" (inflammation, something eating at you). Mila asked - is it her own inability to let go? No. Someone else's. She held strong. The trapped emotions she found were absorbed - she had been absorbing her husband's feelings of unworthiness. The body was showing the energetic truth, not the literal diagnosis.

Section 05

When the Client Disagrees

You are not working with their conscious mind. You are working with their higher self. These are not the same thing.

The conscious mind is the part that says "that's not right" and "I don't think so" and "that doesn't make sense." And it will often say these things when the higher self is showing you something exactly right. Your job is to hold your findings - lovingly, firmly, and without apology.

"Thank you for letting me cognitively know that you don't feel that way. But your higher self is saying yes, and I'm going to honor your higher self."

- Mila, to clients who push back

This is not arrogance - it is the practice. Mila explains it through her physical therapy background: when a patient came in for back pain, her hands would intuitively go to the knee. Every time the patient said "there's nothing wrong with my knee." Every time, the knee was where the chain started - a misaligned patella pulling the ITB, destabilizing the glute, throwing off the pelvis. If she had listened to the patient, she would have missed the root entirely.

How to Hold Your Power Gracefully
  • Acknowledge what they said - "I hear you, thank you for sharing that."
  • Redirect to the higher self - "But your subconscious is giving me something different, and I need to honor that."
  • Keep moving - do not debate, do not defend. Continue the session. The light bulb will come later.
  • Invite them to sit with it - "This may not make sense right now. Come back to it in a few days."
  • Trust your muscle testing - one clear strong response is worth more than their opinion.
Confidence is Part of the Healing

When a client sees that you are confident, that you have your work together, and that you are not a pushover - they have more respect for the process. Mila says it plainly: as soon as they see you are not going to be moved by their skepticism, they settle in. And then the real work can begin.

Think of a time you doubted your own reading. What happened when you stayed with it anyway?

Section 06

Practitioner Ethics & Boundaries

This work is powerful. With that power comes responsibility - to your clients, and to yourself.

Energy work without ethics is energetic trespassing. You cannot enter someone's energy field without their permission. You cannot do surrogate work on a child without the parent's consent. You cannot "snoop" in someone else's business just because you have the skill to do it.

Permission is Non-Negotiable

Before you connect to anyone's energy field - ask. Even in a live workshop demonstration, Mila asks: "Do you give me permission to hook on to your energy field?" This is not a formality. It is the foundation. You do not enter without it.

Energetic Boundaries
  • Always get explicit permission before connecting
  • Do not do remote work on someone without their knowledge
  • Clear your own energy field after every session
  • Know when a session is complete and disconnect cleanly
  • Protect yourself before each session, not after
Knowing When Not to Work
  • If the 5 checks come back unsafe - stop
  • If you sense something is off before you even begin - trust it
  • If a client needs medical care, refer them on
  • If someone is on significant mental health medication - do not work on them
  • A refund and a kind goodbye is always the right call when it is not safe
Animal and Child Surrogate Work

Working through a surrogate requires an extra layer of permission. For children - always get explicit consent from the parent or guardian before connecting to the child's energy. For animals - the owner's permission is required. This is not just ethics, it is energetic protection for you.

Mila's Practitioner Contract with Herself

Every session, Mila puts out this intention: "To do no harm, ever. That the higher self guides and leads me. That anyone who is not for my highest and greatest good simply does not show up." This is not passive - it is an active energetic boundary she sets with intention.
