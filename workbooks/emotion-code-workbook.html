The Four Causes of Illness

What makes us well and what makes us sick

Mila teaches that there are four main things in life that can make us well or make us sick. These are the four areas we look at when dealing with the mind, the body, the spirit, and the soul.

  1. 1
    Nutritional A nutritional issue will make you either healthy or not.

    Emotional Emotional energies that get lodged and are not filtered through.
  3. 3
    Structural Physical and structural issues in the body.

Beyond the emotions

There are other energies beyond the emotions that can hold an issue: offensive energies, post-traumatic energies, pathogens, toxicity, misalignments, and color imbalances. Today we focus only on the emotional energies.

Section 02

Permission Protocol

You must ask before entering anyone's energy field

The Rule

Before you can enter anybody's energy field, you have to ask for their permission. Without their permission, your answers will be wrong. You will ask "Is it in column A?" and get yes. "Is it in column B?" and also get yes. That means you are not in - because you never asked for permission.

Permission rules
  • Adults: You must ask the person directly for permission before working on them.
  • Children under 18: You must ask permission of one of the parents.
  • Animals: Permission must come from the owner - not the person caring for the animal. If the person who contacts you is the boarder and not the owner, you cannot get in without the owner's permission.
  • Comatose person: This is a judgment call. Some healers say to talk to the person's spirit - but you must consider whether you are meddling in their karma.

"It's by all the laws of the universe. You can't break the laws of the universe."

- Mila Velazquez
Section 03

Safety Checks

What Mila asks before every single session

Before starting any session, Mila asks a series of safety questions. Dr. Brad does not teach about safety - Mila added this herself.

  1. 1
    Is it safe for me to work with this person? If you get a no, give them their money back and call it a day. Does this person have an entity in their energy field?
  2. 2
    Is it safe for that person to work with me? Some things are not safe to clear - for example, certain issues in pregnant women. They can come back after they give birth.
  3. 3
    Am I meddling in business that is not for their highest and greatest good? You have no right to meddle in somebody else's karma. It is not good for them and it is not good for you.
  4. 4
    Is it karmic for them? If what they are going through is karmic, clearing it could do more harm than good.

Section 05

Muscle Testing, Proxy & Surrogate

How we communicate with the subconscious mind

We speak to the subconscious mind by using the nervous system and manually muscle testing. You can also use a pendulum or the sway test to test yourself.

Three testing methods
Sway Test

Stand relaxed. Make a true statement - your body sways forward. Make a false statement - it sways back. This is your baseline yes/no.

Arm Test

When they hold strong, it is a yes. When they go weak, it is a no. Strong means congruent. Weak means non-congruent.

Finger Test

Form a ring with your thumb and finger. Try to pull it apart while making a statement. Ring holds = yes. Ring breaks = no.

Proxy Testing & Surrogate Testing
The AM / FM Analogy

If you want to work on someone else - an animal, a child, another person - Dr. Bradley says you take proxy. It is like one person is on AM and the other is on FM. You both have to come together into one frequency, one channel, so one person can test for the other.

Dr. Brad does this with intention: "My intention is I'm going to test on behalf of Chris." When he muscle tests, he says "My name is Mila" - he goes weak. "My name is Chris" - he goes strong. That is proxy.

Mila's approach is different

Mila does not take on the other person's energy. When you take on someone's energy and you see many people in a day, you are exhausted by the end. In her Advanced Healers course, she teaches how to connect to someone's higher self in the quantum field to get the same information without absorbing their energy.

You can also test through a surrogate - hooking two people up together so one person can be tested on behalf of the other.

Finding & Releasing Emotions

The step-by-step process Mila teaches

  1. 1
    Set your intention Call upon your angels, spirits, guides, higher self, God, the universe. Ask for your team and the other person's team to come together. Clear your space. Use elements - fire, water, air, earth.
  2. 2
    Ask permission and run safety checks Ask for permission. Check if it is safe for you and safe for them. Check for entities. Check for karma.
  3. 3
    Check willingness Are they willing, ready, capable, and do they feel safe?
  4. 4
    Ask broad questions first Where is the energy? Is it in the aura? The physical body? Is it a relationship issue? A parenting issue? Money? Career? Start broad and go in - like being a detective.
  5. 5
    Use the chart to find the emotion Column A or B? Even or odd row? Which specific row? Test each emotion until you hold strong.
  6. 6
    Check if the subconscious needs more information If yes, go back through ages: Prenatal? In utero? At birth? 0-10? 10-20? Narrow down to the specific age (within 2 years is a good window).
  7. 7
    Ask if it is safe to clear If not safe, find the emotion underneath that needs to be cleared first.
  8. 8
    Release the emotion Clear through the governing meridian (runs from the forehead over the head and down the back). You can use your hand, a magnet, a dowser crystal, intention, breath work, bells, sound frequency. There is no wrong way.
  9. 9
    Continue until complete Ask: "Are there any other trapped emotions in this category?" Keep going until you get a no. Then ask: "Are you hiding something from me?" Check past lives if needed. Continue until 100% released.
The beauty of the emotion code

You do not have to remember the event in order to clear the energy. With the Neuro-Emotional Technique, you have to remember the event cognitively. Why put someone through the same trauma again if they have already repressed it?

Section 08

Three Types of Trapped Emotions

Personal, absorbed, and inherited - three distinct categories

Personal

Your own emotion from your own experience. "Is it yours? Yes." This is the most straightforward - it happened to you and got stuck.

Absorbed

Someone else's emotion that you took on. "Was it absorbed? Yes. From whom?" You can trace it - was it from mom, dad, your husband, a sibling? That person was in that emotional state and you absorbed it.

Inherited

Passed down through generations. If you go through the emotions and go weak on all of them, ask: "Is it inherited?" Then find the emotion again - "Oh, you inherited peeved." How many generations? Trace the lineage through mother's and father's sides.

"People like it. They want to know where they received their shit from. It's important for them. And then they cry because they remember great-grandmother, grandmother."

- Mila Velazquez

Mila gives a story of the cognitive experience: you find the emotion was fear, absorbed from an ex-husband, around age 18. You give the person a story so they understand where they were in life at that point. When the light bulb goes off, you can tell right away that they remember. But they do not have to remember - the energy can still be cleared.

Entities & Dark Energies

Know your limits and protect yourself

Mila is direct about this topic. When someone comes on, she looks at the person and can tell intuitively if there is an entity. Then she decides whether she is going to release it or not.

Mila's approach to entities

If it is safe and the entity can be released, she does it. If it is a demonic energy or something too strong, she will not go there. If she gets a "no" on safety, she tells the person it is not safe, gives them their money back, and calls it a day. She does not scare anyone, but she is honest.

Key points from Mila
  • A disincarnate (someone who passed on and got stuck in another person's energy field) is something simple to release and send into the light.
  • A really nasty energy - you cannot just do it through distance, no matter what anybody says.
  • If someone is drunk or on drugs, Mila feels it and knows it. That is not something she will work with.
  • You have to know what you can do and what you are not willing to do.
  • Entities love fear. You cannot be afraid of it. If you are afraid of it, you cannot deal with it.
  • Your angels, spirits, and guides are there to protect you. Every healer has a contract.
  • You have to make sure 100% it is safe for you to go into someone's energy field - that they do not have something that will pass from them to you, because it is energy.

Processing After a Session

What happens when the energy leaves - from Mila's experience

Mila tests at the end of each session how many days the person needs to process. Processing means the energy is integrating - it is gone, it is out of the system, out of the energy field. People feel it emotionally, mentally, physically, or spiritually.

The numbers

Dr. Bradley Nelson says that 60% of people process and feel it. 40% do not feel a single thing. Everybody processes differently, and everybody has a different time frame.

Processing symptoms Mila has observed
Crying Some people cry for days at a time. This is the emotion completing its exit.
Uncontrollable laughing An unexpected release that comes through joy or relief.
Itchiness / restlessness The body physically reacting to the energy shift.
Vomiting / diarrhea Physical purging as the body releases.
Shivering / feeling cold Mila's own processing symptom. She had a client sitting by a fireplace saying "this isn't helping" - and the energy was processing. Like running a fever where nothing makes you warm.
Fatigue / wanting to sleep People get tired. Some sleep for hours. One client slept for three hours and woke up happy.
Aggression in dogs Dogs can bite and become aggressive during processing.
Tantrums in children Children can become fussy and throw tantrums. Sometimes they just want to lay down and sleep.
Feeling lighter

People physically feel lighter. They feel relieved. We are releasing negative energies that no longer serve and elevating the vibration of a person. It is all about frequency and vibration.
