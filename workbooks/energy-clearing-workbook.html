What is Energy Clearing?

Understanding how emotional energy gets stored in the body

Every emotion you've ever felt creates an energetic imprint in your body. Most of the time, that energy moves through and releases naturally. But sometimes - during moments of overwhelm, trauma, or times when it wasn't safe to feel something fully - that emotional energy gets stuck. It becomes what we call a trapped emotion.

Trapped emotions aren't in your head. They have a real, physical location in your body. And over time they can interfere with your physical health, your emotional state, and the patterns that show up in your relationships and your life.

Energy clearing is the process of identifying exactly which emotions are trapped, where they're stored, and releasing them - gently, permanently.

Where emotions commonly lodge in the body
1  Grief & Sorrow Chest, lungs, upper back - the physical weight you feel when your heart is heavy
2  Anger & Bitterness Liver, jaw, neck - why your jaw clenches when you're frustrated
3  Fear & Anxiety Kidneys, adrenals, lower back - your body's survival wiring
4  Heartbreak The Heart Wall - layers of emotional protection that can block love and connection
   Shame & Guilt Stomach, solar plexus, throat - the energy of "not enough"

Your subconscious mind holds the map. It knows exactly what's stored, where it lives, and when it got there. That's what we're accessing during a clearing session.

How Trapped Emotions Affect You

A self-assessment - be honest, this is just for you

Trapped emotions don't announce themselves. They show up as symptoms - physical, emotional, relational. Take a moment and tick anything that resonates. Be honest. This is for you.

Check any that apply to you
  • Chronic pain with no clear physical cause - it's there, the scans are "normal"
  • Anxiety or panic that seems to come from nowhere
  • Persistent fatigue and exhaustion, even after rest
  • Difficulty in relationships - patterns that keep repeating
  • Feeling stuck, like you're spinning your wheels no matter what you do
  • A heaviness you can't explain - not sadness exactly, just... heavy
  • Recurring patterns showing up across different areas of life
  • Physical tension that won't fully release no matter how much you stretch or massage
  • Emotional reactions that feel out of proportion to what's actually happening
  • Low self-worth or a persistent inner critic
  • Difficulty feeling joy, ease, or safety in your own body
  • Physical symptoms that have followed you for years with no resolution
What did you notice?

Write down anything that stood out - or how many you ticked, and how that felt.

If you ticked even two or three of these, there's trapped emotional energy contributing. That's not a character flaw. That's just what happens when we're human and we feel things we don't always have the space to fully process.

The Clearing Process

What happens during a session - step by step

Energy clearing follows a specific sequence. Each step matters. Here's what happens during a session - including the one we're doing together today.

Governing meridian - forehead to tailbone

  1. 1
    Connect with the subconscious mind We establish communication using muscle testing - your body's built-in feedback system. Your subconscious responds with yes/no signals through muscle response.
  2. 2
    Identify the trapped emotion Using the Emotion Code chart, we systematically narrow down exactly which emotion is trapped. Your body knows. We're just asking the right questions.
  3. 3
    Locate when and where it became trapped The subconscious can tell us your age when this emotion got stuck, and sometimes the circumstances. This creates context - not so we re-live it, but so the release is complete.
  4. 4
    Release using magnetic energy We use a magnet (or the hand's natural magnetic field) drawn along the governing meridian - from the forehead, over the crown, and down the spine. Three strokes typically releases the emotion.
  5. 5
    Confirm the release We test again. Your body will confirm when the emotion is gone. Sometimes you'll feel it leave - a breath, a lightness, a shift in your chest. Sometimes it's quiet. Both are valid.

You don't have to understand exactly how this works for it to work. I didn't believe in it either until I tried it. Your body knows what it's doing.

Workshop Exercise 03

Self-Clearing Practice

A real tool for releasing emotional energy at home

You can do a simplified version of this work at home. This technique won't replace a full session, but it's a real tool for releasing emotional energy when something feels stuck. Use it whenever you feel heavy, anxious, or like you're carrying something you can't shake.

The technique
  1. 1
    Name what you're feeling Don't filter it. "I feel heavy." "I feel angry." "I feel like I want to cry and I don't know why." Say it out loud if you can. Your subconscious mind needs to know you're working with it, not against it.
  2. 2
    Intend to release it Say (out loud or internally): "I intend to release this feeling from my body, permanently and completely."
  3. 3
    Stroke the governing meridian Using your fingers or the palm of your hand, stroke from your forehead, up and over the top of your head, and down the back of your neck. Do this three times in one continuous motion. You can use a magnet if you have one.
  4. 4
    Check in Take a breath. Notice if anything has shifted - even slightly. Sometimes it's dramatic. Sometimes it's a very quiet "oh." Both are valid.
  5. 5
    Drink water Always. Energy work requires hydration for your body to process the release. This is not optional - your body needs it.

3 strokes, forehead to tailbone

When to use this
  • Before a difficult conversation
  • When anxiety spikes suddenly
  • After a stressful event
  • When you feel physically heavy
  • Before sleep

Section 05

After Your Clearing

What to expect in the 24-48 hours after a session

Your body just let go of something it's been holding for a long time. Give yourself grace. The next 24-48 hours matter. This isn't the time to push through and pretend nothing happened.

Drink water

More than you think you need. Energy clearing stirs things up and your body needs hydration to flush it out. Aim for at least 2 litres today and tomorrow.

Rest without guilt

If you feel tired, lie down. A nap after a clearing is common and normal - it means your body is processing. This is not weakness. This is healing.

Journal what came up

Use the journal pages at the back of this workbook. Write while it's fresh. You don't have to make sense of it - just get it out of your head and onto paper.

Be gentle with yourself

Skip the intense workout. Avoid alcohol today. Don't schedule anything stressful if you can help it. Your nervous system just did real work.

What you might experience

Post-clearing experiences vary. All of the following are normal signs your body is processing a release.

  • Tiredness or emotional fatigue - Your body worked hard. Rest is the right response.
  • Emotional waves - Tears, laughter, or a sudden feeling of relief. Let it move through.
  • Vivid dreams - Your subconscious mind continues to process during sleep. This is good.
  • Feeling lighter - A real, physical lightness in the chest or body. This is what release feels like.
  • Temporary physical symptoms - A headache or mild soreness can occur. It passes within a day or two.
  • Increased clarity - Things you've been wrestling with may suddenly feel obvious or resolved.

"Give yourself at least 48 hours before you decide if it worked. The shift isn't always immediate - sometimes it's the day after when you realise you just... don't feel that thing anymore."

- Mila Velazquez, MSPT, CECP
