What is Energy?

Understanding the unseen life force that flows through all living things

Energy is an unseen life force that flows through all living things. It flows through space, objects, and environments. Everything is a vibration.

At every given point in time, you give off a vibration. Every action, every emotion, every thought, every event leaves behind some kind of energy.

"To clear the energy means to release what doesn't belong to you, to extract, to release and to cleanse in order to restore the flow and the balance back into your energy field."

- Mila Velazquez

Just like cleaning your home and your body, you clean yourself energetically. Your home has an energy to it, too. If we want to be in balance and harmony, energies that are no longer for our highest and greatest good need to go.

Key Concepts
  • Everything is energy - we are energy, other people are energy, situations are energy
  • Energy is vibration - actions, emotions, thoughts, and events all leave energy behind
  • Clearing = releasing what doesn't belong, extracting, cleansing, restoring flow and balance
  • You clean your personal space, your home space, and your work space energetically
  • Energy cannot be created nor destroyed - it can just change form

You're a receiving antenna. You receive energy and you give off energy. The whole point of this work is to keep your mind open and allow yourself to see the unimaginable.

Section 02

Signs Your Space Needs Clearing

How to know when your energy field or your home needs an energetic cleaning

How do we know when our space - our bioenergetic field, or the space that we live in - needs an energetic cleaning? Mila teaches us to use our feelings to sense when something is not right.

Check any signs you recognise
  • Feeling off or drained for no reason - You're tired, exhausted, and there's no reason behind it
  • Heaviness when you walk into a room - A subtleness, like you can feel the density even if you don't know what happened there
  • Increased arguments in the home - No one's getting along, there's tension, there's stress - the space needs to be cleared
  • Feeling emotionally stuck or scattered - A sign that something is off and you need to find balance and alignment
  • Not sleeping well at night - Your room and your space need clearing
  • Electronics are glitchy - Space needs to be cleared
  • After having people over - Even loved ones. Protect your space before they arrive and clean it when everyone leaves
  • Illness in the house - Any kind of illness or emotional distress happening in the home
  • Pets unsettled or acting strange - Pets are very sensitive to energy, including entities
  • Feeling disconnected from your intuition - The space needs to be cleared and you need to ground

Mila's personal sign: "When I feel stuck and frustrated and I feel like I'm scattering all over the place, that's my sign that something is off. I clear my space, I work on myself a little bit, I ground, and then I recheck again."

How to Clean Your Energy

Cleaning your energy field is a spiritual practice that should be done every day

  • Grounding - A daily routine. Relieves stress and releases the energy you're carrying. Great as a starting point for any clearing work.
  • Smudging your aura - Encompasses fire, air, and earth (the herbs). Use palo santo, sage, wormwood, rue, juniper, cedar, or Russian sage. Use a conch shell with salt on the bottom (the coating is toxic when on fire) and a feather to waft the smoke.
  • Salt baths - Add crystals (clear quartz to amplify), Epsom salt, or magnesium flakes. Set the intention that the salt is clearing your energy and washing everything off.
  • Movement - Jumping, speed walking, dancing, shaking, skipping, humming. Any kind of motion to release energy that is stuck. When autistic children run back and forth, they're releasing pent-up energy - that's their way of moving it out.
  • Yoga and breathing - Breathe things out. Use breath as a tool for releasing energy from the body.
  • Crystals - Wonderful for releasing energy. Can be worn as amulets or placed with intention.
  • Sound tools - Bowls, tuning forks, bells, maracas. Any kind of sound to break up the density and shift the frequency.
  • Hand motions - Use your hands to spin out energy. Motion combined with intent clears energy.

Section 04

Working with the Four Elements

Combine fire, water, earth, and air to clear and protect your energy

Fire

Candles - Beeswax is best. Light a candle with intention before sessions or clearing work. Fire gazing - Whether it's a candle flame or a campfire, gazing into the fire is a beautiful way to clear energy. Put your intention into it. Burning herbs - Combine fire with earth (herbs) to burn and cleanse your space. Mila prefers palo santo, wormwood, and rue for clearing; sage for blessing.

Water

Shower as waterfall - Imagine you're standing under a waterfall, cleansing your body. Visualise the energy washing down the drain. Cut the cords. Salt wash - Use salt with water to wash yourself with the intent of cleaning your space. Spiritual baths - Full immersion with intention. Sprays - Make sprays with essential oils that are protective and cleansing. Spray into your energy field, take deep breaths, and ask and intend to clear your space. Mila makes her own homemade rue spray (boil dried rue, use as a protection spray - it stains, so be careful on surfaces).

Earth

Salt - Use any kind (Celtic salt, sea salt, regular salt). Plants and herbs - Natural tools for clearing and protection. Crystals - Place with intention, use in baths, or create grids. Sand - Another earth element for grounding. Earth is the easiest element to combine with the others to make a powerful concoction.

Air

Breath - Use breath to release energy. Chimes - Change the frequency of your space with sound. Feathers - Used in smudging to waft smoke. Intention - The air element is really the thought behind it. The idea, the intent of what you're doing. Combine intention with the other three elements for the most powerful clearing.

You can combine all three physical elements (fire + earth + air) together with intention. Mila says: "You're taking something and you're putting an intention to get an outcome... to clean your energy space."

Cleaning Your Home

A step-by-step guide to energetically clearing your living space

  1. 1
    Set your intention Call upon your angels, your spirits, your guides. "I ask and intend" - not please and thank you. You are commanding and asking and intending with your thoughts to create what is for your highest and greatest good. Make up your own words - speak them as they come to you.
  2. 2
    Start at the doorway, go clockwise room by room Use a white candle for purity with the intent that any negativity gets burned up and out. Smudge with palo santo, sage, mugwort, copal, frankincense, rue, patchouli, wormwood, juniper, or cedarwood. Get the smoke into the corners.
  3. 3
    Keep windows CLOSED while smudging Kill off the energy first, then open the windows to let it out. Exception: if working with entities, always keep a window or door open so the entity has an "out."
  4. 4
    Use the broom Go clockwise around your home, knock on the walls to get stuck energy out. Sweep everything out. At New Year, sweep the house before midnight - anything that doesn't belong in the old year, sweep it out, and go fresh into the New Year.

Rose of Jericho

The resurrection flower - a living energy absorber

The Rose of Jericho is a resurrection flower - a living plant that absorbs negativity from your space. Mila describes it as looking like "a little bird's nest" when dry, and it opens into a green plant when placed in water.

How to Use the Rose of Jericho
  1. 1
    Set it up Place the dry plant in a basin with water. Add copper coins (pennies) to the water.
  2. 2
    Let it open It opens into a green plant and begins soaking in the energy around it.
  3. 3
    Daily care Talk to it, thank it for what it's doing, and change the water every day. If you don't change the water, it goes stale.
  4. 4
    When you're done Take it out of the water, allow it to dry, and it goes back to its bird's nest form. It stays like this forever until you put it in water again - reusable indefinitely.
Best uses
  • Basements (especially those without windows)
  • Offices and treatment rooms
  • When guests are coming over - put it in water to soak up the extra energy
  • Any space that needs ongoing passive clearing

Available on Amazon. Mila used to keep one on the floor in her office and gave them as gifts. When people asked what it was, she'd say "I'm growing something in water."

Pet Safety Note

Mila doesn't know for certain if the Rose of Jericho is safe for pets. Her animals never went near it, but keep it out of reach to be safe - especially with cats who might want to play with it.

Section 08

Animal Safety & Pet Considerations

Be mindful of your animals when doing clearing work

Mila reminds us to be very careful with our clearing materials around animals. Pets are sensitive to energy, but they're also sensitive to the physical tools we use.

Rue is Toxic to Dogs

If you use rue in any form - dried, sprayed, or burned - keep it well away from dogs. This is a serious safety concern.

Essential Oils & Sprays

Be very careful with essential oils and sprays around animals, especially cats. What's safe for humans can be harmful for pets.

Know Your Animals

Some animals get into everything. Keep clearing materials out of reach. If they can't access it - like up on a sofa or high shelf - that's safer.

Clearing for Pets

You can clear energy for your animals too. Pets are very sensitive to all kinds of energies, including entities. If your pet is acting strange, the space needs clearing.

When in doubt, research every herb, oil, and material you plan to use around your animals before you use it. Their safety comes first.
