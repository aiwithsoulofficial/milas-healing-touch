The Sway Test

Your body as a living pendulum - learning to listen and allow

The sway test is one of the most accessible forms of intuitive testing. Your entire body becomes the instrument. When you stand still and relaxed, your body will naturally lean toward truth (yes) and away from untruth (no). It's subtle. It's real. And it takes practice to trust.

"Allow the body to respond. Don't make it perform."

- Mila Velazquez
Step-by-Step Method

How to Perform the Sway Test

  1. 1
    Stand upright, feet hip-width apart Plant yourself. Feel your feet on the ground. You want stability, not rigidity.
  2. 2
    Relax your shoulders - loose but not sloppy Arms by your sides. Drop the tension. Breathe.
  3. 3
    Close your eyes if comfortable This helps you tune into the subtle movement without visual distraction.
  4. 4
    State "Show me yes" - observe which direction your body moves For many people, forward = yes. But not always. YOUR body will show YOU.
  5. 5
    State "Show me no" - observe the opposite direction Backward = no for most. But some people sway sideways. There's no wrong answer.
Important

Forward = yes and backward = no works for many people, but NOT always. Trust YOUR body's unique response. The key is to observe, not to force.

Part 03

Pendulum Testing

Amplifying subtle energetic information through a physical tool

The pendulum is not magic. It is an extension of you - your intention, your nervous system, your subtle energetic communication, and your intuitive guidance. It amplifies what your body already knows, making the invisible visible through movement.

Key Principle

Each person's pendulum language is DIFFERENT. There are no universal rules. Your pendulum might swing clockwise for yes, counterclockwise for no - or it might swing forward-back, side-to-side, or in a completely unique pattern. You must calibrate YOUR instrument.

Step-by-Step Method

How to Use a Pendulum

  1. 1
    Hold the chain between your thumb and finger Let the pendulum hang still. Elbow supported on a table or steady in air.
  2. 2
    Take a breath and centre yourself Clear your mind. Release any expectation of what the answer "should" be.
  3. 3
    Say "Show me yes" - observe the movement It may swing clockwise, counterclockwise, forward-back, or side-to-side. Just observe.
  4. 4
    Say "Show me no" - observe the opposite Your pendulum should move in a distinctly different pattern from your "yes."
  5. 5
    Say "Show me maybe" or "Show me unclear" This gives you a third signal - essential for honest, accurate readings.

Testing on Another Person

The arm test method - permission, boundaries, and gentle listening

Testing on another person is a privilege. It requires permission, a clear explanation of what you're doing, respect for their body, gentle handling, and complete neutrality. You are not there to prove anything. You are there to listen.

"You are not there to dominate their body. You are there to listen to it."

- Mila Velazquez
The Arm Test Method

Basic Arm Test - Step by Step

  1. 1
    Person holds their arm straight out to the side Shoulder height, palm facing down. Comfortable, not strained.
  2. 2
    They resist gently as you apply light downward pressure Tell them: "Hold firm but don't fight me." Light pressure only.
  3. 3
    True statement = muscle stays stable and strong The arm holds. This is a "yes" response from the body.
  4. 4
    False or stressful statement = muscle weakens or unlocks The arm gives way. Not because they're weak - because the energy field changed.
Critical Reminder

NEVER be aggressive with muscle testing. Too much force distorts the results entirely. This is about subtlety, not strength. If you're pushing hard, you're doing it wrong.

Supplements & Remedies

Testing whether a substance is beneficial for the person at this time

Surrogate Testing

Testing at a distance through consciousness and energetic connection

Surrogate testing means testing through one person on behalf of another - a person, an animal, or a child. This method is used when the subject is not physically present, when a child is too young to be tested directly, when testing for an animal, or when someone is too weak to participate.

The foundation of surrogate testing is this: consciousness and energetic information are NOT limited by physical distance. If this feels like a stretch, remember - Mila does this work remotely every day, with clients across the world. The results speak for themselves.

Step-by-Step Method

How to Surrogate Test

  1. 1
    Centre and ground yourself Take several deep breaths. Clear your mind. Be fully present.
  2. 2
    Ask permission "Do I have permission to surrogate test for this person/animal?" Use your testing method. If no - stop. Honour the boundary.
  3. 3
    Clearly identify who you're testing for State their name. Be specific. "I am now connecting with [name]'s energy field."
  4. 4
    Connect only with their field for the highest good Set the intention. This is not casual - it's sacred. You are being entrusted with someone's energetic information.
  5. 5
    Use your chosen method (sway, finger, pendulum) Proceed with your testing, interpreting the responses as belonging to the person you're connected with.
Critical Ethical Boundaries

Surrogate testing is NOT for:

  • Spying on someone or checking up on them
  • Bypassing consent or testing without permission
  • Satisfying curiosity about someone else's private information
  • Any purpose that is not for the highest good of the person being tested

"That is not spiritual wisdom. That is energetic boundary crossing."

- Mila Velazquez

Teach this method with maturity and integrity. The ability to access someone's energy field is a responsibility, not an entertainment. Approach it with the same seriousness you'd bring to handling someone's medical records.
