You always want to start with an intent

How Mila connects

  2. 2

Asking the right questions

Questions should be concise, direct, precise. The more specific you are, the better your answers will be.

Vague question

"Should I go on vacation?"

Well, sure you should. Why not? - That's too broad to get a useful answer.

Specific questions

"Is it for my highest and greatest good to go on vacation?"

"Is it for my highest and greatest good to go on vacation in July?"

"Is it for my highest and greatest good to go to Florida? In August?"

Trust what you get

Trust what you get is what you get instead of asking the same question different ways and seeing if you get a different answer. What you get is what you get - with no questions.

Method 01

The Sway Test

The easiest way to start - a human pendulum

How to do it
  1. 1
    Stand up. Take a few deep breaths. Relax your arms by your side. Clear your mind.
  2. 2
    Deep breath in through your nose, out through your mouth.
  3. 3
    Say "My name is [your name]" and see which way your body goes. It's a very subtle motion.
  4. 4
    Then say something that's false - "My name is Henrietta" - and notice the opposite direction.
  5. 5
    Test with more questions you already know the answer to: "I live in [city]," "I have [number] kids." Say some things that are true and some that are false, just to check which way you're going.

Important: Sometimes a yes is forward and no is back. Some people are opposite. Some people go side to side. This is why we always want to get a baseline before we start.

↑
Forward = Yes
↓
Backward = No
Stand loose - it's a very gentle, subtle motion
Seated sway

You can do this in a seated position if you can't stand. It's a little bit harder, but it works.

Practical uses

If you're using supplements, you hold the supplements and you can ask, "Is this for my highest and greatest good?" and see which way you go. You won't be knocked off your feet - you'll just gently be pulled. There is no thinking behind it at all.

Method 02

The Pendulum

Anything that's hanging on a string - you can make your own

How to use it
  1. 1
    Hold the pendulum any way you want. Mila holds it up so people can see.
  2. 2
    Ask "Show me my yes" - it will swing in one direction (forward/back, circle, etc.).
  3. 3
    Ask "Show me my no" - it will show a different motion.
  4. 4
    Ask "Show me none of my business" - you'll get a third response (for Mila, counterclockwise).
  5. 5
    You can even ask for a "jumping yes" - a big, emphatic response.

Your arm will move. That's because your nerves are firing to keep your shoulder up and your elbow flexed while holding the pendulum. Your arm is going to move because you're holding the pendulum in your hand and your fingers are firing. That's normal.

It can go forward/back
It can circle
It can go diagonal
It can stand still
Why Mila doesn't use it in sessions

"I don't use it when I work because this is very slow to do with someone, you know, when asking questions. I'm impatient and I got to get in and I got to get out. But I do use my pendulum for other metaphysical things like clearing energy."

Cross-check your methods

You can always use the pendulum against your sway test. If you do the sway test, you can check it against the pendulum. Same principle - you hold it, you relax your hands, there's no thought about it, you calibrate, then you ask questions.

Method 03

Two-Handed Methods

The O-Ring, the hole-in-one, and the duck

The O-Ring (Ring and Ring)

The hardest method to do, according to Mila. Make two rings with your fingers. One hand is the holder, one is the puller.

  • Hold them nice and gentle - it's not a death grip
  • Decide which hand holds, which pulls
  • Put them inside together, ask a question
  • True: fingers stay strong
  • False: fingers open up

It's very gentle - you don't pull for dear life.

The Hole-in-One

Mila's preferred two-handed method because it's multifaceted - you can use it in any position, with any finger.

  • Make a hole with one hand (thumb + finger)
  • Put one finger from your other hand through it
  • Whatever finger you feel like putting in
  • True: stays strong
  • False: goes weak

You can hold it any way - thumb and finger, different positions. Very versatile.

The Duck Method

"I don't prefer the duck. You make a duck with a duck, and you ask a question. I don't like this one. This one takes too much of my time and space and my fingers." - Mila demonstrates it but doesn't recommend it.

Mila's tip on switching methods

"I use different types depending on which finger is hurting. Arthritis. So depending on where I am, if I'm underneath my desk and I get tired, I'll do these to test. Then I'll do these. When I get tired, I go to the one hand."

Method 04

One-Handed Methods

The flick, the roll, the snap, and finger-over-finger

Mila recommends learning one-handed methods because "the one-handed comes in handy." There are plenty of options.

The Flick Method

MILA'S FAVOURITE - THE ONE SHE USES TO WORK ALL THE TIME

You can use whatever finger you feel like. Mila uses her index finger or her third finger with her thumb.

  • True: hold strong (smooth flick)
  • False: go weak (can't flick cleanly)

"I know a therapist - she uses her pinky. That's the only thing she uses, and she is fast with that freaking finger. I wouldn't use the pinky, but to each his own."

The Roll Method

Hold one finger on top of the other, and one starts to move - a rolling motion.

  • Congruent: it easily moves, continues to roll
  • Non-congruent: it will get gritty - it just doesn't go

"This is a really good one." - Mila

The Snap Test

If it's a yes, you snap easily. If it's a no, it gets caught.

  • True: clean snap
  • False: fingers catch / stick

"This is not one that I prefer to use. I catch myself making mistakes with the snap test because I really like to snap my fingers." - Mila

Finger-Over-Finger

One finger stabilises on the bottom. Your index finger presses down on top.

  • True: press down, stay strong
  • False: go weak

"To me, this is very hard to do. My fingers don't want to go in this direction. But people that do this, they do this like so quick." - Mila

Also possible: wrist and bicep testing

You can manual muscle test on the wrist - Mila says "this is not a muscle that I like, but you can." You can also test on your biceps muscle, or on somebody else's biceps. For testing on someone else, you will normally use their shoulder muscle because it's a stronger muscle.

Tongue Clocking

You clock with your tongue along the roof of your mouth.

  • Yes: a strong clock - smooth movement
  • No: it gets stuck in the roof of your mouth

Lip Pursing

You purse your lips together.

  • Yes: you can easily purse your lips
  • No: it goes into a funky shape - it's not a pursing

The Swallow Method

Mila's pick of the mouth methods for being truly inconspicuous.

Permission & Ethics

Without someone's permission, you can't get into their energy field

The core rule

"Are you there to snoop around in somebody else's business? It doesn't work that way. Because without someone's permission, you can't get into their energy field. You'll get bogus answers."

Mila's permission rules
  • If you're working on someone else, you have to ask their permission first
  • You can't just use your free will - there are energetic boundaries and spiritual boundaries that we hold
  • If it's an animal, you have to ask permission of the owner
  • If a puppy just gave birth, you have to ask the mother dog for permission - not just the human owner
  • Same with a horse, cat, whoever
  • You can ask yourself what you think about someone, what your higher self thinks about them - but you cannot ask them what they think, without their permission
  • You can't be snooping in somebody else's business
Testing on another person

When you manually muscle test on someone else, there are important requirements:

Your job (the tester)

Your job and your only job is to ask the question and manually muscle test. You use the shoulder muscle normally, because it's a stronger muscle. You stabilise the shoulder, put the arm on, and give pressure.

Their job (the person being tested)

The other person's job is to match your pressure. Not to anticipate, but to match the pressure that you're giving them. If it's true, the arm will stay strong. If it's false, the arm will go weak.

Make sure they're ready

You have to make sure that they're in the clear, that they're switched on, that there's no issues in their energy field. That they're hydrated, that they've used the bathroom. You can switch arms - right arm or left arm, it makes no difference.

Real examples of manual muscle testing in action

Leo the Dog

When Mila took Leo to the vet and got bad news, she sat in the car while they did the ultrasound. She aligned, connected to her higher self, connected to Leo's higher self, and asked him directly:

"Are you dying?" He said yes. "Can you benefit from this surgery?" He said no. "Is there something I can do to get you better?" He said no. "Is it for your highest and greatest good to leave you alone?" Yes. "Is it for your highest and greatest good to go home?" Yes. "You want to go home?" Yes.

The doctor wanted to open him up, but Mila knew from his file he had an inflammatory bowel and couldn't have anti-inflammatories or antibiotics. She was on the third floor with no elevator. "You're going to open him up and then do what with him? He's 10 years old."

She changed his diet using manual muscle testing - "How about some turkey?" He said yes. "How about we boil everything to boil out all that fat?" He said good idea. She asked which supplements he needed and went with whatever he said.

"The doctor said to me, he's not going to live past two, Mila. He's a really sick dog. And I said, stop right there. And look, he's almost 10 years old. Yes, he's been a sick dog. But a happy and a healthy one at that."

Troubleshooting & Building Intuition

When it's not working, what to check - and what manual muscle testing is really for

When you get the same response for true and false

A workshop attendee asked this exact question. Mila's answer:

You might not be fully connected

Maybe you're not fully connected to your higher self or grounded in. Go back to your sacred space.

You might be thirsty

"Or you're thirsty. It's hard to do." Drink water. This fixes more problems than people think.

Create your sacred space

"Light a candle, smudge, clear the energy around you. You can't have other people running around in the other room screaming."

It takes practice

"Don't forget when it's new, you have to practice. You're going to make sometimes wrong answers. This is why you're double checking."

What manual muscle testing is not for

"Manual muscle testing is not meant to know the future. Will I get married to this gorgeous guy in a year? He's loaded with a lot of money? Yeah, sure." - Use it for guidance and clarity, not fortune-telling.

What it is for
Finding balance and clarity

"Using the manual muscle testing on yourself can give you a little bit of balance, a little bit of clarity. It gives you the ability to have a conversation with your higher self that knows everything, your subconscious mind, your memory. It helps you dig through some of the issues that you might have to find some issues."

Intuition is the real destination

"It's using your intuition. It's closing down your logical reasoning, left-sided space and time brain. And it's accessing that right side of your brain, where your intuition lives, where your creativity lives, where your languages live. It's innately inside of you. Intuition is subtle.
